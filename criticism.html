Criticism and Research

Criticism

Shakespeare has been studied and analyzed for centuries. In the past few decades, in particular, scholars have looked at what his works tell us about race and gender.

Research

If you wish to do some more research, here are some fun databases and digital collections to dig around in

  • The NYPL's New York Shakespeare Festival's digital collection of photographs
  • Open Source Shakespeare allows you to search the collection of plays, including doing a keyword search of all the plays
  • The Folger Library's Online Collection of its materials
  • The Folger Library's API means you can explore the plays to find out weird and fun things, like who is on stage in any play for the any line in a specific play, or all its sound directions
  • The World Shakespeare Bibliography allows you to search Shakespeare scholarship from 1960 onward, including productions and digital projects
