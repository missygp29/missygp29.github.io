Shakespeare Gardens

Shakespeare Gardens feature plants that are mentioned in his works. You often find them in botanical gardens, like in the Brooklyn Botanic Garden, or in a park, like Central Park in New York City.

The New York Botanical Garden has a guide to some of the things you can plant if you want to create a Shakespeare garden. Here are some of the quotes from plays and associated plants, as seen at the Brooklyn Botanic Garden. (All photos by Grace Pickering, 2024).
