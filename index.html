Crosswording

Hello! Welcome to Crosswording. Here, we look at the rather particular world of British Cryptic Crosswords. Niche, admittedly, but doing crosswords expands your vocabulary, oils your brain, and is an excellent way to while away your time.

Why Crosswording?


There are many types of delightful crosswords out there but, here, when I talk about crosswords I mean British cryptic crosswords - the kind published daily that uses a shared vocabulary and techniques for solving them; there is an established language of cryptic clues, and this site is about how we solve them and why they are my favourite form.
I grew up doing cryptic crosswords; learning how to do them is something my mother taught me; it's something I want to teach my own children; it is a bond between my stepmother and me; and is one of the pastimes that calms me by engaging my brain and sense of curiosity.
