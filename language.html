Shakespearean Phrases

We use a huge number of phrases today that were first recorded or originated in Shakespeare's plays. "Star cross'd lovers"? Romeo and Juliet. "In stitches"? Twelfth Night. "Break the ice"? Taming of the Shrew. Here are a couple of more comprehensive lists.

"Translators"

There are a bunch of translators online that claim to put modern sentences into Shakespearean language OR to translate Shakespeare into modern youth slang, including side-by-side translations of the plays. You be the judge on their success...

Shakespearean Insults

Want to take your insulting to another level? Stand out from the crowd? Shakespeare is famous for his insults! No matter what you're trying to say, it's likely that Shakespeare has an insult for you.
