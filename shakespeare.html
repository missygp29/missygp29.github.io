How to Read Shakespeare

This section has some tips and tricks on how to read Shakespeare.

Shakespeare isn't easy for our modern ears. If you have not been taught how to read him, it's no fun. In addition, it's arduous to look up every other word. But if you can get into the rhythm and flow of his work, it's a rich marvel of language. So, here are some tricks and tips to help us learn how to understand Shakespeare's words.

Shakespearean Insults

Want to take your insulting to another level? Stand out from the crowd? Shakespeare is famous for his insults! No matter what you're trying to say, it's likely that Shakespeare has an insult for you.

You can, of course, discover these by reading the plays. But, if that's not working for you right now, you can always use one of the Internet's already made insult lists.

Alternatively, some ingenious people have put together random Shakespeare insult generators, putting together various insulting words found in Shakespeare, then into new combinations.
