Why Shakespeare?

Reading and knowing more about Shakespeare taps into a couple of things that are important for young and new adults.

First, a commitment to learning, particularly reading for pleasure.

Second, if you find a love for Shakespeare, it can open a doorway to creative activities, such as working in the theater or poetry.
